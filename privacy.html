Privacy Policy

You may use Fantasy Ultimate's services in a number of different ways - to follow players, keep track of current games, compete against friends, and more. By sharing your information with us in making a Fantasy Ultimate account, you help us present you with the most pertinent content and important news and updates. As you use our services, we want you to be clear how we're using information and the ways in which you can protect your privacy.

What We Collect

Upon registering for an account, we collect your name, email, and a password that you will use for your account login. All passwords are encrypted on our secure server. We nor anyone else has knowledge of or access to your directly provided (unencrypted) password.

How We Use Your Information

Your name is used for the purpose of your peers finding you on our site as well as basic identification features when you are engaging in challenges and games. Your email is used for site notifications (such as challenge invites and game outcomes) along with the occasional service update email. Your name, associated email, or any other information collected upon registration is not shared with any third party or affiliate. We do not use your information for marketing or other solicitation related purposes.

Changes

Our privacy policy may change from time to time in order to accommodate new features or services. We will not conduct any revocation of rights nor lift any restrictions without first explicitly informing you of the planned changes. If you wish, you may terminate your account at any time and we will discard completely of any and all data associated with your account.

What You Can Do

To unsubscribe from our service emails, navigate to the account page and go to the "Edit" tab. Under the "Notifications" option, unselect "Enable Emails." If you have any further concerns pertaining to your privacy or the integrity of your data, please contact us and we will work toward resolving the issue with you.
